my story

My work engages play, empathy, and analytics to devise solutions to wicked problems. I spend time with people, tools, materials, and ideas until I know their truth. Then I use these discoveries to create human-centered services and environments, with an eye towards emerging technologies such as machine learning, VR & AR, and ambient displays. I have found my dramatic practice as a storyteller and creative constantly informs the moments I orchestrate throughout my work.

At Carnegie Mellon University, I study Video & Media Design in the School of Drama and Human-Computer Interaction in the School of Computer Science. I've done research with Min Kyung Lee on services for the homeless and Niki Kittur & Jeff Rzeszotarski on the touch-based data visualization tool Data Squid.

Last summer, I worked as a UX Design Intern at Procore Technologies, a leader in construction management software, to redesign aspects of the implementation and onboarding process for new accounts. I collaborated closely with Sales & Customer Success to create seamless frontstage and backstage interactions. I also redesigned the architecture of the platform's menu system and lay the groundwork for an Amazon Echo integration.

I've designed and created multiple dramatic works, including most recently Milk Like Sugar at CMU. I'm the Associate Director for Design & Curation at Round Room Image, where I've collaborated on Surrounded and Surrounded 2.0 (the latter was devised in Scotland and presented at the Prague Quadrennial).

In my work with the Carnegie Leadership Consultants, I've created multiple workshops to educate the student community about leadership and help individual organization's align with their goals. I'm a member of CMU's IMPAQT program, which establishes meaningful ties between the Pittsburgh and Qatar campuses.

interaction design + drama

Are you wondering how my theatre background has informed my work as an interaction designer? Well here you go!
world building
Every production lives in its own fictional world the creative team constructs. I love bending the rules of reality by creating abstract and poetic spaces. I bend the same rules when imagining future technologies.
storytelling
Whether the medium is words, graphics, interfaces, movement, or stage effects, I construct narratives that allow a range of people to understand my ideas.
ideation & prototyping
I think on my feet and toss out radical ideas, in rehearsal and at the whiteboard. I know the value of making quick prototypes to learn about an idea on its feet.
leadership
Whether it's a stage crew weilding wrenches or a design team with Post-Its in hand, I know how to lead people through fast-paced and complex endeavours.
sequencing & timing
I have a natural sense for structuring and pacing experiences, whether that's cues for fight sequences or intimate service interactions.
improvisation
I'm not afraid of being put on the spot, whether its in meetings or in brainstorming sessions. I know that the best ideas often come from "exsquisite pressure". I have a toolkit of exercises to get creative juices flowing.
